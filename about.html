About Me.

Who am I and what do I want?

I am a trailblazer. “Be comfortable being uncomfortable” is the best piece of advice I was given by a professor, and it has become my mantra. I want to blaze a path past my boundaries, assumptions and comfort zone. Only when I find myself in uncomfortable situations will I be able to discover self-improvement. This means putting myself out there, and doing things for the first time. Most people fail when doing things the first time, and I’m no exception. However, I want to persist through my failures and find the satisfaction of a job well done. Only when I persist through my failures will I be able to find success.

Life is a journey. Finding self-improvement and success is proof that I am moving forward on this adventure.

Currently, I am about to graduate from UMBC.
As this segment of my journey comes to an end, I am now seeking new challenges.
